Nerala is downloadable anywhere and at any time and all platforms via app store , play store and on its official website.

Installation is straightforward. Simply click on the downloaded app to install Nerala. Enjoy all its services and content on your mobile devices, and tablets.

To activate all the features in Nerala, you need to subscribe to the Premium plan.
